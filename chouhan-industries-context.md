## What Chouhan Industries is

Chouhan Industries is an open source hardware manufacturing company, founded 2025. Website: https://chouhan.ai

It designs and manufactures precision consumer hardware and publishes everything about it — schematics, tolerances, materials, and manufacturing processes — without restriction. The goal is consumer products you *truly own*: fully open, customizable down to the source, and yours to build from the files, adapt to your needs, or simply buy finished.

**Founding conviction:** the best hardware should be free for anyone to study, build upon, and improve.

## The philosophy

- Hardware is too important to be kept closed.
- The manufacturing industry has long run on opacity: proprietary designs, closed tolerances, undisclosed processes. Chouhan Industries treats that model as unnecessary and actively harmful to the pace of human progress.
- Premium quality and full transparency are not in conflict. You can build things that last, share everything about how you built them, and still hold the highest standard.
- Open source wins because **transparency scales and trust does not.** A closed system asks you to take its word; an open one hands you the schematics and invites you to check. That difference compounds — bugs surface sooner, security holds up under real scrutiny, and every improvement builds on the last instead of starting from zero.

## How the open source commitment works

- **Open by default.** Every schematic, tolerance spec, and process document is public record. No proprietary lock-in, no licensing walls.
- **Release policy.** Design files are published *before* units ship. No product is released without first publishing everything needed to reproduce it independently. Nothing you buy is ever a black box.
- **License.** All hardware designs are released under **CERN OHL-S v2.0** (Strongly Reciprocal). Anyone can use, study, modify, and distribute the designs, provided modifications are released under the same license. This is a legal commitment that the work stays open, permanently. (Website code itself is MIT.)
- **Repository, contributing, docs.** Design files, schematics, and documentation live on GitHub (https://github.com/animesh-chouhan). Contributions from engineers, manufacturers, and researchers are welcome via pull requests and issues; all discussion is public. Manufacturing tolerances, assembly processes, and materials specs are published alongside the design files.
- Aligned with the Open Source Hardware Association (OSHWA) definition of open source hardware.

## Capabilities

1. **Open Source** — every schematic, tolerance spec, and process document is public record.
2. **Manufacturing** — full-stack production across mechanical, electronic, and systems hardware, every step owned in-house.
3. **Quality Assurance** — testing is not a phase, it is the process. Every unit meets the same standard, without exception.

## Products

| Product | Status | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| **Open Media Platform** (flagship) | In Development | A fully open, modular media computing platform. Every input, output, codec, and expansion module is replaceable and documented. Built for any medium, any format, any use case. |
| **Open Telegraph** | In Development | A pocket-size Morse key. One button, one RGB LED, an ESP32-S3, and a battery. Plug it in over USB-C or pair over Bluetooth and it types Morse into any device as a standard keyboard. |
| **Open Clock** | Planned | A fully open, configurable clock. Choose the accuracy source (NTP, PTP, GPS, or atomic), the display panel (analog or digital), and the power supply (solar to always-on AC). Every combination is documented and built to order. |
| **Open Purifier** | Planned | A fully open, modular water purifier. Choose the filtration stages (sediment, carbon, reverse osmosis, UV); a monitoring core reads the water going in and coming out. Every cartridge is documented, refillable, and free of DRM. |

All design decisions are made in public. Progress is tracked on the build log (blog) and the GitHub repository.
