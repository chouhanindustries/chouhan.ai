## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Public investor page exists and is reachable

The site SHALL provide a public investor-oriented page at a stable URL (`/investors.html`), reachable from the site chrome (at least the footer).

#### Scenario: Investor page is reachable

- **WHEN** a visitor uses the footer (or nav)
- **THEN** a link leads to the investor page, which loads with the shared site layout

### Requirement: The four-part moat and USP are presented

The investor page SHALL present the business thesis that openness is customer acquisition and manufacturing is margin, and SHALL articulate the four-part moat: manufacturing execution, canonical-source trust, the reciprocal license as a one-way valve, and compounding community velocity.

#### Scenario: Moat and thesis are present

- **WHEN** an investor reads the page
- **THEN** the "openness is acquisition, manufacturing is margin" thesis and all four moat elements are stated

### Requirement: Competitive asymmetry and tailwinds are argued

The investor page SHALL argue that the company competes on an axis incumbents structurally cannot follow (ownership, repairability, verifiability), starting in segments giants neglect, and SHALL cite real tailwinds (advancing right-to-repair regulation; institutional demand for auditable hardware).

#### Scenario: Asymmetry is explained

- **WHEN** an investor reads the competition argument
- **THEN** the page explains why incumbents' proprietary model prevents them from copying the approach, and names the regulatory/market tailwinds

### Requirement: Public-safe, routes to private conversation

The investor page SHALL NOT publish the specific raise amount, round milestones, or precise traction figures. It SHALL instead route interested investors to a private conversation (for example a contact email).

#### Scenario: No private figures, clear next step

- **WHEN** an investor finishes the page
- **THEN** no raise amount, milestone commitments, or exact traction counts are shown, and a contact path for a private conversation is provided

### Requirement: Honesty and brand voice

Claims on the investor page SHALL be honest and accurate (no false process-knowledge tenure; precedents and competitor claims accurate) and SHALL use the site voice with no em-dashes.

#### Scenario: Claims hold up

- **WHEN** the investor page is reviewed
- **THEN** it contains no fabricated tenure or numbers, represents precedents accurately, and reads in the site voice without em-dashes
